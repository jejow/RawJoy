Product Details

Made with carefully selected ingredients, this formula supports your pet’s daily health while staying gentle on sensitive stomachs.

  • Supports fresher breath naturally
  • Helps reduce plaque buildup over time
  • Promotes healthy gums and oral hygiene
  • Made with human-grade, clean ingredients
Nutritional Info

Active Ingredients (per 5g scoop):
- Natural Seaweed Blend – supports plaque reduction
- Parsley Leaf – helps freshen breath naturally
- Turmeric Root – supports healthy gums
- Coconut Powder – promotes oral balanceGuaranteed Analysis:
- Crude Protein (min): 12%
- Crude Fat (min): 4%
- Crude Fiber (max): 6%
- Moisture (max): 8%
Made without grains, artificial preservatives, or synthetic additives.

Feeding Guidelines

Mix directly into your dog’s regular food once daily.

Under 25 lbs (11 kg): ½ scoop
25–75 lbs (11–34 kg): 1 scoop
Over 75 lbs (34 kg+): 1½ scoops

For best results, use consistently as part of your dog’s daily routine.

Always provide fresh water.
If your dog is pregnant, nursing, or under veterinary care, consult your veterinarian before use.
